Cré inov'
Une offre de parcours intégré pour le créateur d'entreprise innovante

Created in 2002 by University of Lille 1 ,Cré 'Innov is an incubator of innovative companies. It is intended to promote and to develop the creation of innovative companies in relation with the skills and scientific resources of the University's laboratories and training courses. Since 2002, more than 220 projects studied and supported, 52 companies have been created with a survival rate of 80%.
